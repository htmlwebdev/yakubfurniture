Why Choose us

Yaqub Furniture, located in Mayur Vihar Phase 3, Delhi, has been selling and distributing furniture since 2009. We specialise in retailing furniture and home décor for homes and families. We also provide office furniture for commercial areas and businesses. Our company, which has been in operation for many years, is one of the city's most prominent points-of-sale for purchasing upscale and fashionable furniture.

We provide a product line that is in line with current interior design trends and styles. The items have helped them develop a sizable consumer base over time due to their high quality, fashionable designs, and inexpensive pricing points. Yaqub furniture is a household name in Mayur Vihar Phase 3.
